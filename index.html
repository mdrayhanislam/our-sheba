Frequently Asked Questions

Destination events are our favorite! We've worked with people from the East Coast to the West Coast, and even internationally! We have been able to share our "Southern Approach" wherever we go. Our team is very comfortable handling communication from afar to help us stay on track and we completely understand the unique dynamics of handling busy work schedules, travel schedules, and the fun things that come with this type of event. Whether it's assisting with flights and transportation, or planning fun excursions - we're here to help make it happen for you, your family, and friends.

It's true, we do specialize in weddings, however, our team offers a variety of planning services for the following:

  • Corporate Events - Seminars, Conferences, Galas, Holiday Parties, and more!
  • Private Events - Birthday Parties, Anniversary Parties, Baby Showers, etc.
  • Community Events - Non-Profit Functions, Charity Events, Fundraisers, Fairs/Festivals, and more!

    Not everyone offers this service, but we believe it's imperative to give our clients another option, which is why we've created our "Accountable Approach". This approach is for couples who want a higher level of assistance throughout the planning process, but still want to plan the event mostly themselves. We’re here to hold you accountable by assisting you through the entire process!

    Our long history and experience within the event industry has given us the opportunity to build strong relationships with vendors we know and trust. We want to make sure you have the best experience and that is why we will always give our best vendor recommendations for your needs and budget.

    Our primary goal is to help you stay focused and organized while planning, and to ensure your event goes off without a hitch. We're here to fully support YOU, to help you stay on track, refer you to reputable vendors, supply a wealth of resources, and negotiate on your behalf to carry out your vision. We give you the peace of mind you need and the desired outcomes you deserve.

    Your Venue Coordinator/Manager’s job is to oversee the venue and make sure everything goes smoothly on-site. We act as the overall Event Manager, we like to see ourselves as the CEO of your wedding day. Our main job is to make sure YOU and all of your guests are happy at every step of the way, and that ALL vendors are providing you with the best experience.

    Our process is simple!

    1. Meet and Greet - Let's book a date to get a chance to get to know you, hear your story, grasp your vision, and share a little about us to see if we're a good fit for each other.
    2. Deeper Dive - Now that we've heard your story and have a better idea where you want to go, we’ll go over our service options and figure out which approach best fits your needs.
    3. The Right Fit - We've gotten to know you a little more and have a better understanding of your personality and your overall vision of what you want and need for your upcoming journey. Let's pair you up with the right planner so you'll be able to fully enjoy the planning process with together!
    4. Planning Palooza - Ok, here we go! Get ready to dive right in and get the party started! Our team is fully prepared to take you on a fun and exciting journey and get into the nitty gritty of the details. Let's start knocking some tasks out and get the ball rolling!
    5. Execution Day - Our favorite part of what we do! This is the day that all the hard work and effort pays off. We want you to embrace this day and not to worry about a thing - we've got your back and will work tirelessly to make this day one to remember for the rest of your life!
